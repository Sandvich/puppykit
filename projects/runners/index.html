Runners' Hub

The Runners' Hub is a small dynamic website to help co-ordinate a Dungeons and Dragons Organised Play - style Shadowrun campaign.

In Progress

Aim

The goal is to run a campaign at Imperial College London which will function in a similar way to Dungeons and Dragons Organised Play. Each session, a number of players turn up with characters that they've been playing for the entire campaign. They group themselves into teams, play a mission in a self-consistent universe, and get rewards.

I aim for the site to allow players to:

  • Choose which mission they want to play before a session begins.
  • Keep track of their contacts (including the other players).
  • Receive character rewards privately - in case they screwed someone else over!
  • Track downtime, karma and nuyen spent.
  • Upload a character bio and image.

Meanwhile, GMs will be able to:

  • Create missions.
  • Create NPC bios.
  • Assign characters to PCs as contacts (by setting Connection and Loyalty).
  • Upload documents, handout style.
